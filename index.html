About Fredrik

Built from real

Fredrik Emanuelsson spent years in finance and business before realising that the most valuable things he'd learned — about money, careers, and life — were never taught in school.

So he wrote four books. Then built a platform. Then trained an AI to help scale the conversation to every classroom.

"Everything starts with you. Not your circumstances, not your school, not your parents. You."

The Starts With You series is already published and in hands of young people across Portugal and beyond. This platform brings it to life.
